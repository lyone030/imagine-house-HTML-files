The Space

Two floors. One mood.

A 3,800-square-foot duplex with original wide-plank hardwood, twin skylights, cast-iron columns and an open mezzanine. The room shifts effortlessly from a seated dinner of fifty to a standing reception of one hundred and fifty.

  • Footprint3,800+ sq ft, duplex
  • Capacity50 seated · 150 reception
  • LightTwin skylights · south-facing windows
  • SoundTuned in-room system · sound team
  • CateringOpen kitchen · prep pantry
  • AccessibilityStreet-level · freight entrance
  • AVIn-house production

What We Provide

Full production in-house.

Imagine House is a self-contained venue. We bring partners for every production need, or work with yours. See the Food & Beverage page for more info on our in-house team.

Catering & F&B

Open kitchen, prep pantry, curated list of chef partners from Michelin-starred to low-key supper clubs. Beverage programming and natural wine available.

Sound & AV

Tuned in-room speaker system, vinyl rig, full AV production for keynotes and screenings. DJ and live music setups available.

Lighting & Décor

Full lighting rig with dimmer control. We work with preferred floral and décor partners, or integrate your creative direction.

Staffing

Event management, on-site coordinator, waitstaff, security and front-of-house team. Day-of production support included for all bookings.

Press & NDA

Confidential intake. NDAs honoured on request. We host embargoed press events, private previews and invitation-only activations regularly.

Hold Dates

Hold options available 6–9 months out. Co-curation available for cultural programming. Replies within 48 hours on all inquiries.
